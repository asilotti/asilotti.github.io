About

I have an engineering degree and ten years as an Account Executive in software sales. That combination shapes how I approach everything: I think in systems, build toward outcomes, and communicate in terms that actually land with customers. It's a different way of working than most people in either field.

These days I apply all of it to AI building. I'm not a developer, and I never pretended to be one. What I bring is a decade of knowing what moves people, what problems are worth solving, and what separates a good idea from something that actually ships. Turns out that matters more than knowing how to code.

I also coach and mentor early-career sales professionals. I've helped people land offers at some of the best SaaS companies in the industry and make meaningful jumps in their earning potential. I know what the path actually looks like, and I'm not shy about sharing it.
